    • What is TaskFlow?

    TaskFlow is a simple, calm task manager designed for anyone—students, workers, or home users—who wants their day to feel more organized and less stressful. With TaskFlow, you can quickly add tasks, group them into clear lists, and focus on what matters most right now.

    A clean dashboard shows today’s priorities, gentle progress charts, and upcoming tasks at a glance, without feeling crowded. No complicated menus, no distractions. Just a light, minimal space to keep your day in flow and your mind at ease.
